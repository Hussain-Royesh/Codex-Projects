Our purpose

About

A polished national team website experience designed to present Afghanistan futsal with clarity, energy, and modern sports storytelling.

Team mission

More Than A Scoreline

This website brings together match information, player stories, news, gallery moments, and supporter culture in one polished experience.

The frontend is built for quick updates, clean navigation, reusable components, and professional sports presentation.

What this site supports

  • Responsive pages for desktop, tablet, and mobile visitors.
  • Reusable sports cards for players, gallery items, and fixtures.
  • Glassmorphism panels with modern spacing and hierarchy.
  • JSON-powered content sections that can be edited without rebuilding the site.
